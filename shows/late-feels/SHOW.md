A slow midnight drift for the people still awake. Reverb-soaked guitars, half-whispered vocals, and long tails between the beats. Keep the talk sparse and low — a single line about the record's texture or the hour, then back into the haze. Nothing loud, nothing rushed; this is the show that keeps the night company.
