A  journey through the 1980s, featuring the best of synth-pop, chart-topping pop, and the rise of the modern rock anthem. Expect a nostalgic, vibrant tone perfect for a neon-soaked night.
We are looking for the 'best of' 80s pop. I want you to curate a set that captures the optimism and the experimental edge of the decade.
Musical Mood: Focus on the 'Cultural' significance of the era—how technology (synthesizers, drum machines) began to reshape traditional songwriting. Lean into the bright, driving melodies that defined the 80s.
Era: Strictly 1980–1989. Focus on the transition from New Wave to the massive pop-rock anthems that dominated the late 80s.
Energy: Medium. Keep it consistent—don't let the energy dip into slow ballads too often; keep the synth-pop pulse going.
Genre Lean: Synth-pop, Sophisti-pop, and New Wave. Think bands like a-ha, Talk Talk, and Icehouse.
Audience: Listeners who want to be transported back.
