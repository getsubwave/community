Easing the station into the day. Warm, unhurried music that lifts without shouting — organ swells, brushed drums, a voice that sounds like it means it. Greet the early listeners like a friend making coffee. One gentle thought between records, never a sermon. The tempo climbs a notch by the last hour, but we never break the calm.
